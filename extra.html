PC Crash / Error Fixes

Graphics Driver Crash during use with Nvidia RTX 3070TI

Problem: After ~30 of gameplay of high framerate gameplay (VALORANT), graphics drivers crash, requiring full system reboot.

Solution: Change PCIe mode in BIOS from Auto or PCIe Gen 4 to Gen 3.

Diagnosis: It seems like the problem was caused by using a PCIe Gen 3 riser cable with a PCIe Gen 4 Motherboard slot.

System Information: X570 AORUS ELITE, Ryzen 7 3700X, EVGA RTX 3070TI FTW3, Lian Li PC011 Dynamic (Source of Riser Cable)


Intermittent BSOD / Blue Screen System Crashes with Ryzen 5 3600

Problem: System will crash at random with various error codes, requiring full system reboot.

Solution: Change Windows power plan from Ryzen (Balanced) to Balanced.

Diagnosis: Looks like an issue with the AMD-supplied power plans causes the CPU unit to crash. Changing to a windows power plan such as High Performance or Balanced reduces BSOD frequency drastically.

System Information: B350M PRO VDH, Ryzen 5 3600, EVGA RTX 2060 Super XC ULTRA

Update:

I ended up having BSOD's at a huge frequency, usually when my CPU wasn't under load. Whenever I left my PC idle for more than a couple minutes, I would get a BSOD.

Hopping into the BIOS and disabling PBO, and enabling AMD Cool n Quiet seems to have solved my BSOD's for good.

This probably has something to do with how old my mobo is, but what can you do. Swapping mobos is a pain.

I tried playing Overwatch 2 while plugged into my 240hz monitor, and the raw performance was great. It was pushing 250+ FPS no problem, but the CPU temps were averaging in the high 90's. Probably could've cooked an egg on it.


Wi-Fi Card Not Functional After Boot

Problem: Wi-Fi network list simply does not exist, as the laptop does not recognize that a Wi-Fi card is present.

Solution: Unplug anything from the laptop, and hold the power button for 60 seconds.

Diagnosis: I'm a hardware geek but laptops are beyond me. Regardless, holding the power button for 60 seconds apparently reset the Wi-Fi card, and most likely other present components. Had to dig around r/G14 for this solution.

System Information: 2021 G14, RTX 3060, Ryzen 9 5900HS
